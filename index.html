What We Do

Our Services

From digital door locks to hotel-grade security — Homex Digital Services provides complete smart lock solutions with professional installation, sales, and after-sales support.

Digital Door Locks

Fingerprint, PIN, RFID and app-controlled digital door locks for homes, flats and apartments. Installed by certified technicians.

Mechanical Door Locks

Premium quality mechanical locks for main doors and security doors. High-durability mortise and rim locks with anti-pick protection.

Office Locks

Smart access control and digital locks for offices, cabins, server rooms and restricted areas. Multi-user management and audit trail.

Digital Safes

Home and office digital safes with PIN and biometric access. Fireproof and waterproof options available for documents and valuables.

Commercial & Hotel Locks

Card-based and master-key system locks for hotels, guesthouses, commercial complexes and large properties. Bulk supply available.

Installation, Sales & Service

End-to-end solution — we supply genuine products, install professionally, and provide ongoing after-sales service and annual maintenance.
